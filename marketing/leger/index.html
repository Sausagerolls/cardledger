Features

Everything a trader needs, nothing they don't.

01

📸Log every card with photos

Snap the card with the camera or pull from your library. Record the purchase price, condition, set, quantity and notes — for Dragon Ball, Pokémon, Magic, Yu-Gi-Oh! or any game you set up.

02

💷Instant resale pricing

Set a target profit and CardLedger shows the price to list at, with VAT factored in and your real profit after tax. Your tax rate and method live in Settings.

03

🔖Short codes & QR for every card

Each card gets a tidy code like DBF-7K3Q and a QR. Scan or type it to jump straight to the card — perfect for labelling sleeves and binders.

04

✨Auto-fill from card databases

Search by name or card number and pull the set, rarity and official art automatically — each game wired to its own source. No typing out every detail.

05

☁️Backed up to your iCloud

Cards and photos sync privately to your own iCloud. Nothing lives on our servers — your collection is yours, safe if you lose or change your device.

06

🖥️View & edit on any computer

Turn on Desktop mode and open your inventory in a browser on the same Wi-Fi — Windows or Mac. Browse, add and edit cards from a full-size screen and keyboard.

07

📊Export to a spreadsheet

One tap exports a clean CSV of your whole inventory — open it in Numbers, Excel or Google Sheets to total up, sort and report.
